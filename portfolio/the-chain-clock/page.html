The 'chain' clock

November, 2016

This data visualisation shows all of the bands/artists that have made five or more appearances on BBC Radio 6 Music's 'the chain'. Each band/artist is represented by a record, proportional in size to number of appearances, and positioned on a clock according to the year they began their career ('scraped' from Wikipedia). Hover or touch the records to list the bands/artists for a given year.
